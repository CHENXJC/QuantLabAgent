# Disclaimer

QuantLab Agent is an educational, research, and portfolio demonstration project.

This project does not provide financial advice, investment advice, trading advice, or personalized portfolio recommendations.

All outputs generated by this project, including signals, watchlists, backtest results, candlestick observations, risk scores, and AI-generated summaries, are for research and demonstration purposes only.

---

## No Real Trading

QuantLab Agent does not execute real trades.

The project does not place orders, connect to brokerage accounts for real-money execution, or manage real investment portfolios.

---

## No Guaranteed Returns

Past performance does not guarantee future results.

Backtesting results are based on historical data and may not reflect real market conditions, liquidity constraints, slippage, fees, taxes, or execution risk.

---

## Watchlist Disclaimer

Daily Momentum Watchlist and Long-Term Candidate Pool outputs are research watchlists only.

They should not be interpreted as buy, sell, or hold recommendations.

---

## Data Disclaimer

Market data may be delayed, incomplete, inaccurate, or subject to data provider limitations.

The project may use public or free data sources during the MVP phase. Such sources are suitable for educational and demonstration purposes but should not be treated as institutional-grade market data.

---

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for their own financial decisions.

Anyone considering investment or trading decisions should conduct independent research and consult qualified financial professionals where appropriate.
